## 长难句拆解练习
今日选取2024年考研英语（一）阅读Text3经典长难句进行专项训练：
> The researchers found that children who were exposed to more screen time before age 5 had lower scores on tests measuring cognitive development at age 8, even after accounting for factors such as parental education and family income.

### 结构拆解：
1.  主句主干：The researchers found + 宾语从句
2.  宾语从句主干：children had lower scores
3.  后置定语修饰children：who were exposed to more screen time before age 5
4.  介词短语作状语：on tests measuring cognitive development at age 8（其中measuring...为现在分词作后置定语修饰tests）
5.  让步状语：even after accounting for factors such as parental education and family income

### 参考翻译：
研究人员发现，即便在控制了父母受教育程度、家庭收入等干扰因素后，5岁前接触屏幕时间更长的儿童，在8岁时的认知发育测试中得分更低。
