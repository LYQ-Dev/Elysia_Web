## 长难句拆解
今日选取2025年全国硕士研究生招生考试英语（一）Text 3中的经典长难句进行系统分析：
> The researchers argue that because reduced sleep has been linked to impaired decision-making, a decline in academic performance and even weight gain, the policy of cutting school start times to allow more sleep could have widespread benefits for students’ well-being and academic outcomes.
### 语法结构拆解
1.  主句框架：`The researchers argue + 宾语从句`
2.  宾语从句嵌套原因状语从句：`because reduced sleep has been linked to impaired decision-making, a decline in academic performance and even weight gain`，其中`impaired decision-making`、`a decline in academic performance`、`even weight gain`为并列宾语
3.  宾语从句主干：`the policy...could have widespread benefits for students’ well-being and academic outcomes`，其中`of cutting school start times to allow more sleep`为后置定语修饰`the policy`
### 参考译文
研究人员指出，由于睡眠不足与决策能力受损、学业表现下滑甚至体重增加存在关联，因此调整上课时间以保障学生获得更多睡眠的政策，或将对学生的身心健康与学业成果产生广泛益处。
