## 长难句解析

> *"Rather than focusing on whether teens are using social media for harm or good, researchers are increasingly turning their attention to the scale of use and how it interacts with other aspects of their lives."*

1.  **结构拆分**：本句主干为`researchers are turning their attention to sth.`，句首`Rather than focusing on...`为介词短语作让步状语；`whether teens are using social media for harm or good`为宾语从句作介词`on`的宾语；`how it interacts with other aspects of their lives`为并列宾语从句，与`the scale of use`共同作介词`to`的宾语。
2.  **重点考点**：熟词僻义`scale`（考研常考“规模、程度”而非“秤”）、短语`interact with`（与…相互作用）
