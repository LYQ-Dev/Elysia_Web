## 长难句精读拆解
选取2024年全国硕士研究生招生考试英语（一）阅读理解Text 2的经典长难句进行练习：
> 原句：Rather than dismissing the trend as a passing fad, researchers argue that it reflects a fundamental shift in the way consumers interact with brands.
> 结构拆解：
> 1.  让步状语：Rather than dismissing the trend as a passing fad
> 2.  主句主语：researchers
> 3.  主句谓语：argue that
> 4.  宾语从句：it reflects a fundamental shift in the way consumers interact with brands
> 5.  定语从句：`consumers interact with brands`修饰先行词`the way`，可省略关系词that/which
> 参考译文：研究人员并未将这一趋势斥为一时兴起的潮流，而是认为它反映了消费者与品牌互动方式的根本性转变。
