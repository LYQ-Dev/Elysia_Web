## 长难句复盘
今日拆解的典型长难句（选自2023英语一Text3）：
> And yet, despite the proliferation of smart home devices that promise to make our lives easier, many of us still struggle to integrate them into our daily routines without feeling overwhelmed.
- 结构拆分：主句为`many of us still struggle to integrate them into our daily routines`；`despite the proliferation of smart home devices`为让步状语，其中`that promise to make our lives easier`是定语从句修饰`smart home devices`。
- 重点语法：`without feeling overwhelmed`为介词短语作伴随状语，`proliferation`在语境中意为“激增、泛滥”，`integrate...into`为固定搭配“将……融入”。
- 参考译文：然而，尽管各类承诺让生活更便捷的智能家居设备层出不穷，我们许多人仍难以在不感到不堪重负的前提下，将它们融入日常生活。
