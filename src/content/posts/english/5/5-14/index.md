## 长难句拆解
### 真题原句（2021年英语一 Text 3）
> Rather than concluding that children make parents either happy or miserable, Senior suggests we need to redefine happiness: instead of thinking of it as something that can be measured by moment-to-moment joy, we should consider being happy as a past-tense condition.
### 语法拆解
1.  开头`Rather than concluding that...`为介词短语作状语，其中`that`引导宾语从句作concluding的宾语
2.  主句为`Senior suggests we need to redefine happiness`，其中`suggests`后接省略that的宾语从句
3.  冒号后内容为对前文的补充说明，`instead of thinking of it as something that...`为另一组状语，其中`that can be measured by moment-to-moment joy`为定语从句修饰something
