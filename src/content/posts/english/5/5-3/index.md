## 单词复盘
### 必考词（Unit 3 重点整理）
1.  **perceive** /pəˈsiːv/ v. 察觉，感知；理解，领悟
    搭配：be perceived as 被视为，被看作
    例句：Researchers found that dogs can perceive human emotions accurately.
2.  **persist** /pəˈsɪst/ v. 坚持，执意；持续存在
    搭配：persist in (doing) sth. 坚持做某事
    例句：Despite multiple setbacks, he persisted in applying for the graduate program.
3.  **sustain** /səˈsteɪn/ v. 维持，保持；支撑，承受；供养
    例句：The team managed to sustain their lead until the final minute of the match.
4.  **perspective** /pəˈspektɪv/ n. 视角，观点；透视画法
    形近辨析：**prospective** /prəˈspektɪv/ adj. 未来的，预期的
5.  **compensate** /ˈkɒmpenseɪt/ v. 补偿，弥补；赔偿
    搭配：compensate sb. for (doing) sth. 因某事赔偿某人

## 长难句解析
### 原句（2023 英一 Text 2）
> But while online reviews, ratings, and recommendations have democratized access to information about products and services, they have also introduced new risks, from algorithmic bias to misleading reviews from paid influencers.

### 结构拆解
1.  让步状语从句：`while online reviews, ratings, and recommendations have democratized access to information about products and services`，其中`about products and services`为后置定语修饰`information`。
2.  主句：`they have also introduced new risks`，其中`they`指代前文的`online reviews, ratings, and recommendations`。
3.  后置定语：`from algorithmic bias to misleading reviews from paid influencers`，用于举例说明`new risks`的具体类型。

### 参考翻译
尽管在线评论、评分与推荐使人们获取产品和服务信息的渠道更加民主化，但它们也带来了新的风险——从算法偏见到付费网红发布的误导性评论。
