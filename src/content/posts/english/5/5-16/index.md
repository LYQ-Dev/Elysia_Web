## 单词复盘
### 今日核心高频词汇
1.  **permeate** v. 渗透，弥漫；遍布
    考研搭配：permeate through 渗透过
    例句：The smell of freshly baked bread permeated the entire apartment.
2.  **skepticism** n. 怀疑态度，怀疑论
    形近词拓展：skeptical adj. 怀疑的；skeptic n. 怀疑论者
3.  **aggregate** adj. 总的，合计的；v. 合计，总计
    考研高频考点：aggregate data 汇总数据；aggregate demand 总需求
4.  **diminish** v. 减少，降低；贬低，削弱
    同义词替换：reduce, decline, weaken
5.  **perspective** n. 视角，观点；透视画法
    常用搭配：in perspective 正确地，合理地；put sth. in perspective 客观看待某事
6.  **supplement** n. 补充物，增补版；v. 补充，增补
    考研语境：diet supplement 膳食补充剂；supplementary reading 补充阅读材料

## 长难句分析
今日拆解的典型考研长难句（选自2018英一Text1）：
> The court, however, ruled that a federal law prohibiting the sale of counterfeit goods does not prevent a company from suing individuals who post online reviews criticizing the product.
1.  句子结构拆解：
    - 主句：The court ruled that...，其中however为插入语
    - that引导宾语从句，从句主干为：a federal law does not prevent a company from suing individuals
    - 后置定语：prohibiting the sale of counterfeit goods 修饰federal law
    - 定语从句：who post online reviews criticizing the product 修饰individuals，其中criticizing the product为现在分词短语修饰reviews
2.  重点词汇：counterfeit adj. 伪造的，假冒的；suing v. 起诉，控告
3.  参考翻译：然而，最高法院裁定，一项禁止销售假冒商品的联邦法律并未阻止公司起诉那些发布批评该产品的在线评论的个人。
