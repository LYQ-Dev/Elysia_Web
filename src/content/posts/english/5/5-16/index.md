## 单词复盘
（AI 自动整理考研高频核心词汇）
1.  **permeate** /ˈpɜːmieɪt/ v. 渗透，弥漫；充满，充斥
    > 例句：The smell of coffee permeated the whole office. 咖啡的香气弥漫了整个办公室。
2.  **disperse** /dɪˈspɜːs/ v. 分散，散开；传播，散布
    > 例句：The wind dispersed the clouds after the storm. 暴风雨过后，风吹散了乌云。
3.  **rigorous** /ˈrɪɡərəs/ adj. 严格的，严谨的；缜密的
    > 例句：We need to conduct a rigorous analysis of the data. 我们需要对数据进行严谨的分析。
4.  **deteriorate** /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪt/ v. 恶化，退化；变坏
    > 例句：The patient's health deteriorated rapidly after the surgery. 手术后患者的健康状况迅速恶化。
5.  **mitigate** /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/ v. 减轻，缓解；使缓和
    > 例句：Measures should be taken to mitigate the impact of climate change. 应采取措施缓解气候变化的影响。

## 长难句拆解
选自2025年全国硕士研究生招生考试英语（一）阅读Text 2真题原句：
> The researchers from the University of California and Stanford University found that the way people use their phones in social settings could significantly affect their feelings of connection and overall well-being.
1.  **结构拆分**：
    - 主句主干：The researchers... found + 宾语从句
    - 后置定语修饰主语：from the University of California and Stanford University
    - 宾语从句主干：the way... could significantly affect their feelings of connection and overall well-being
    - 省略关系代词的定语从句修饰`the way`：people use their phones in social settings（完整结构为`the way in which people use their phones...`）
2.  参考翻译：加州大学与斯坦福大学的研究人员发现，人们在社交场合使用手机的方式，会显著影响他们的社交联结感与整体幸福感。
