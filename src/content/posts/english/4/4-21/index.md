## 单词复盘
### 核心高频词巩固
1.  **ambiguous** /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/
    释义：adj. 模棱两可的，含糊不清的
    真题搭配：ambiguous policy guidelines 模糊的政策指引；an ambiguous diplomatic statement 模棱两可的外交声明
2.  **coincide** /ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/
    释义：v. 同时发生；相符，一致
    真题搭配：coincide with one's expectations 符合某人的预期；The team's victory coincided with the national holiday.
3.  **conventional** /kənˈvenʃənl/
    释义：adj. 传统的，常规的；熟词僻义：adj. 保守的，守旧的
    真题例句：Many young people reject conventional social norms. 许多年轻人摒弃传统社会规范。
4.  **diminish** /dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/
    释义：v. 减少，降低；贬低，削弱
    真题搭配：diminish one's reputation 损害某人的声誉

## 长难句分析
今日拆解的长难句来自2023年英语一Text2：
> The researchers argue that a paper's total impact depends not just on the number of citations it receives but also on the range of journals in which those citations appear.
### 句子拆解
1.  主句：The researchers argue + 宾语从句
2.  宾语从句主干：a paper's total impact depends not just on A but also on B
    - A: the number of citations it receives（定语从句it receives修饰citations）
    - B: the range of journals in which those citations appear（定语从句in which引导，修饰journals）
