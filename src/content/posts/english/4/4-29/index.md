> 原句：The companies that Digital Group runs would not only provide digital services but also help governments to enforce surveillance measures that restrict personal freedom.
> 结构拆分：
> 1.  主句主语：The companies，定语从句`that Digital Group runs`修饰主语
> 2.  主句谓语：`would not only provide...but also help...`并列结构
> 3.  第二个并列谓语`help`的宾语：governments，宾补：`to enforce surveillance measures`
> 4.  后置定语从句`that restrict personal freedom`修饰`surveillance measures`
> 参考译文：数字集团运营的这些公司不仅会提供数字服务，还会协助政府实施限制个人自由的监控措施。
